About Ashlee

Designed for
real life

I design homes that truly work for the people living in them.

Before becoming a designer, I spent nine years as a primary school teacher — a career that shaped how I anticipate needs and translate them into practical, thoughtful solutions. I'm also a wife, a mother, and a proud owner of two rescue cats, so I understand firsthand how a home needs to function beyond just looking good.

Originally from Canada, I've been based in Dubai for over seven years. After completing my Design Diploma at The Designer's Studio here in Dubai, I've personally renovated multiple properties across the UAE — particularly within the Springs community — and understand these homes intimately.

"I'm not interested in making a space look good on paper — I want it to work effortlessly for the people inside it."

Organisation isn't an afterthought in my process — it's the foundation. Because when a space is truly organised, it almost takes care of itself.
